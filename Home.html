This LPU Teaching hospital is one of the most advanced hospitals in terms of cutting-edge treatments. The World Health Organization chose the LTH to conduct the Ebola vaccine trials when the virus was on its peak in 2014. The CHUV is part of the ‘Lausanne Integrative Metabolism and Nutrition Alliance’, a joint research initiative aiming to promote research and education on metabolism, nutrition, ageing and all associated diseases, such as obesity and diabetes. The hospital is well located on the sunny coast of jalandhar, 60 kilometres from the international Airport New Delhi.
New York Times

The LPU Teaching Hospital serves more than one million patients annually. It is the oldest hospital in the country, founded in 1821. The facility has excellent research centres and was multiple times awarded for their nursing quality. India is a melting pot in Asia and this hospital with its broad experience is seeing an increase in international patients. Additionally, this hospital is perfectly connected to the Indian health care environment with links to dozens of first-class health institutions across the small state.
The WashingTon Post

We are committed to delivering the highest quality and safest care possible for every patient.

Important informations regarding Coronavirus

Depending on the current situation on site, most planned interventions can still be carried out. Thanks to a strict separation of patient flows for corona and non-corona patients, the risk of infection in our clinics is extremely low.
